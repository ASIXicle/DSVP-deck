## FAQ

**Can I use DSVP for personal/internal use without a commercial license?**
Yes. The GPL v3 permits unrestricted personal and internal use. A commercial license is only needed if you distribute DSVP as part of a proprietary product.

**Can I modify and redistribute DSVP under the GPL?**
Yes. That's exactly what the GPL v3 allows, provided you also release your modifications under the GPL v3.
